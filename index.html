Biography

asd;flkjsDavid Casbeer is the Team Lead over Cooperative & Intelligent UAV Control with the Control Science Center of Excellence, Aerospace Systems Directorate, Air Force Research Laboratory, where he conducts and leads basic research involving the control of autonomous UAVs with a particular emphasis on high-level decision making and planning under uncertainty. He received B.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Brigham Young University in 2003 and 2009, respectively. He currently serves as a Senior Editor for the Journal of Intelligent and Robotic Systems and an Associate Editor for the AIAA Journal of Aerospace Information Systems.
